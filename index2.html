Our Academic Pathways

At Blossom Gates Academy, we currently offer programs from Pre-Nursery to Primary School, fostering a strong foundation based on the Nigerian Education System.

Core Educational Levels

Pre-Nursery & Nursery (Ages 2-5)

Building essential social, cognitive, and motor skills through play-based learning and early literacy:

  • Literacy & Phonics
  • Numeracy & Shape Recognition
  • Practical Life Skills & Sensorial Activities
  • Outdoor & Imaginative Play

Primary Education (Basic 1-6)

A solid academic foundation built on the core Nigerian Primary curriculum:

  • Mathematics & English Studies
  • Basic Science & Technology (BST)
  • National Values Education (NVE)
  • Pre-Vocational Studies & Cultural Arts

Digital & Character Development

Integrated programs to prepare our young learners for the modern world:

  • Early Computer Literacy (ICT)
  • Character & Moral Instruction
  • French/Other Foreign Language Introduction
  • Creative Writing & Storytelling

Enrichment Programs & Clubs

Information & Communication Technology (ICT)

A hands-on program tailored for primary learners to build essential digital literacy skills:

  • Fun Coding & Basic Logic Games
  • Safe Internet Navigation & Tablet Skills
  • Digital Art and Creativity Tools

Indoor Games & Clubs

Fostering critical thinking, strategy, and teamwork through engaging indoor activities:

  • Junior Chess & Scrabble Club
  • Reading and Book Club
  • Art & Craft Workshop
