About the School

Welcome to Blossom Gates Academy. We are committed to academic excellence, character formation, and the holistic development of our learners. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where every child can realize their full potential, supported by experienced staff and state-of-the-art resources.

Admission Guidelines

  • Age and class placement are determined based on the **Birth Certificate** and a formal interview/test where applicable.
  • Required documents for submission: **Original Birth Certificate** (for verification), **Recent Passport Photograph**, and **Last School Results** (photocopy, where applicable).
  • Please complete all sections of this form and submit it alongside the required attachments to the admissions office during working hours.
